### Overview

In this session, GitHub experts guide participants through the core concepts, benefits, and practical implementation of an Expert Hub.

Participants will learn how to:

- Understand the purpose and structure of an internal Expert Hub
- Identify and empower Copilot Champions within their organization
- Utilize GitHub Discussions to promote collaboration, knowledge sharing, and problem solving
- Maintain security and privacy when handling proprietary information

This session blends education, discussion, and demonstration to help teams confidently launch and sustain their own Expert Hub.

### Topics

- Introduction to the Expert Hub
- Champion Roles and Responsibilities
- Building a Knowledge Base with GitHub Discussions
- Promoting Collaboration and Engagement
- Privacy and Proprietary Information guidelines
- Scaling the Expert Hub Across Teams

### Customer benefits

This offering will help customers:

- Centralize internal Copilot knowledge—eliminating the need to search multiple systems for documentation or policies
- Empower internal advocates to provide accurate, timely answers to their peers’ questions
- Encourage knowledge-sharing while protecting internal or proprietary information
- Sustain Copilot adoption through community-driven enablement and InnerSource principles

### Learning objectives

By the end of this training, learners will be able to:

- Explain the purpose and value of the GitHub Expert Hub
- Identify and develop effective Expert Hub Champions
- Use GitHub Discussions to manage collaboration and community interactions
- Establish guidelines for internal communication and data protection
- Plan for ongoing community engagement and growth beyond the training
