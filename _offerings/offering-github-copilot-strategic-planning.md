### Overview

In this strategic advisory session, GitHub's experts will guide your team through adopting Copilot, highlighting its value and potential return on investment (ROI). 

Participants can expect:

- Copilot’s Value: Quick review and alignment of Copilot’s value to your enterprise.  
- Dialing In: Identify and define measurable outcomes.  
- Theory to practice: Identifying and refining company-specific objectives to effectively track progress and measure success.  
- Review and next steps: Talk about long term plans and future sessions.

Participants will leave this session equipped with a personalized roadmap and actionable timeline for deploying Copilot and measuring its ongoing impact.

### Topics

- Copilot’s value  
- Dialing in: Goal definition  
- Theory to practice  
- Review and next steps

### Customer benefits

The results customers experience from this offering:

- Improved decision-making and team alignment, resulting from a clear, shared vision of the benefits from GitHub Copilot  
- Clear and defined business goals for GitHub Copilot  
- Improved progress and success tracking, derived from GitHub Copilot  
- Enhanced software quality and innovation due to enhanced use of AI-powered assistance  
- Increased developer satisfaction and retention through clearer goals and reduced ambiguity in AI company recommendations

### Goals

After completing this service, customers will:

- Understand the benefits of GitHub Copilot for their specific business  
- Identify their company's top goals for Copilot  
- Determine best measurements and collection tools to meet customer's specific needs to maximize Copilot adoption  
- Measure the outcomes and adoption of Copilot based on customer-specific goals  
- Walk away with a clear, actionable plan for deployment and measurement of Copilot adoption
